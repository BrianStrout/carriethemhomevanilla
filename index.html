Carrie Thomas Animal Rights Activist
Welcome to Carrie Them Home
My collection point for my efforts in the world of animal welfare, both on and offline. I have been involved in coordinating fundraisers, transporting rescues, as well as connecting with rescue organizations and people, to help find foster and forever homes for rescued animals. I have work with legislators to update and change legislation focused on animal welfare, and helped raise community awareness about puppy mills, animal adoption, and the importance of spay/neuter.

Outside of my direct efforts, I contribute funding to qualified animal organizations to help rescue, transport and provide medical services to animals in need.

I am located on the Massachusetts’ shoreline, north of Boston, and I am happy to make connections with people also passionate about animal welfare.

-Carrie Thomas

Resources

Rescue Road Warriors

One of the organizations we support is Rescue Road Warriors. We've transported for this well-run all-volunteer group that transports dogs (and cats) from high-risk shelters to permanent 'furever' homes. This non-profit organization coordinates transport to approved rescue organizations and is always looking for additional transporters. We're glad to help you become a transporter! Click on their Facebook link below to learn more. https://www.facebook.com/makingtheroadtofreedomlessruff/

The Humane Society

Starting in September 2018, I joined The Humane Society of the United States as a volunteer District Leader here in Massachusetts, for the 6th District. I am part of a passionate group of animal advocates from the East Coast to the West Coast, and help in advocating for Humane Laws. Our big initiatives are: Stopping puppy mills Ending Cosmetics Animal Testing Banning Trophy Hunting Protecting chickens used for meat Preventing horse slaughter We're also working on: Battling the worst hunting practices Ending animal fighting Ending dog meat Ending pet homelessness Fighting animal cruelty and neglect Going Fur-free Helping captive animals Improving the lives of farm animals Keeping Pets for Life Promoting Smarter Wildlife Management Protecting Equines Protecting Marine Wildlife Stopping the Wildlife Trade Taking Suffering out of Science Eat Humanely For more information, please visit: https://www.humanesociety.org
